# NeuroTechX@UIUC

## What are we about?
NeuroTech is a student-led organization at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign dedicated to exploring the interdisciplinary study of neurotechnology. This combines the field of neuroscience & technology, fusing the capabilities of the body with the advanced, powerful technology that we have to come to develop and appreciate. We also do outreach to encourage people to learn more about neurotechnology with various programs and medias.
